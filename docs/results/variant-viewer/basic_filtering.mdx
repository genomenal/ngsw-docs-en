# Basic filtering of variants

Basic filtering options are at the top of the table:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/basic_filtering.png').default} width="900"/>
</p>

### Basic filtering options

1. <font size="4"><b>Origin</b></font>: All, Somatic, Germline.
Both here and in all subsequent cases, <b><i>the filtering algorithm</i></b> is as follows:
- Click on the container:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/all.png').default} width="150"/>
</p>

- Select the needed filtering option, or type the option you are looking for in the search box and select from the found.
- Done! Filter applied. The selected filter can be seen in the window (in this case, the "Somatic" origin is selected):

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/set_option.png').default} width="150"/>
</p>

:::info
"Origin" filtering option is hidden in the Basic Filtering mode of [Variant Viewer with germline mutations](/results/variant-viewer/variant-viewer).
:::

2. <font size="4"><b>Gene panel</b></font>.
You can add your own gene panel on the "<b>Settings</b>" -> "<b>Gene panels</b>" page or
import panels from the Library of common gene panels:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/gene_panel.png').default} width="300"/>
</p>

Select the needed panels in the opened window and click on "**Import**":

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/impot_from_library.png').default} height="600"/>
</p>

3. <font size="4"><b>Gene</b></font>: gene transcript (shown in brackets) filtering.

:::info
If several transcripts are known for a gene, the list presents all:
<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/many_transcripts.png').default} height="400"/>
</p>
:::

4. <font size="4"><b>HPO</b></font>: filtering by <a href="https://hpo.jax.org/app/">Human Phenotype Ontology</a>.
To use this filter, click on the container:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/hpo_all.png').default} width="150"/>
</p>

You will see the ontology term search box:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/hpo_search.png').default} width="800"/>
</p>

The drop-down list contains the main term groups.
Вы можете отфильтровать варианты как по целой группе, так и по конкретному термину.
You can filter variants either by the whole group or by a specific term.
The term can be found in the group's list of terms, or by searching by name or ID from [Human Phenotype Ontology](https://hpo.jax.org/app/).
For example, a search for the term “glioma” found several terms:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/glioma_search.png').default} width="800"/>
</p>

Selecting one of them (for instance, "Glioma"), you will see this term page:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/glioma_page.png').default} width="800"/>
</p>

On the right, there are the term definition and a comment.
In the middle, there is the hierarchy of the term: the terms above the selected Glioma
term are its ancestor terms (that is, the terms of the groups that include the Glioma term),
and the terms below Glioma are its child terms.
On the right, there are the genes associated with this term (in this case, 45 genes were found).

If you want to filter the variants by these genes, then click **OK**.
To ***cancel filtering***, click on the cross next to the HPO filtering field:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/cancel_hpo.png').default} width="150"/>
</p>

5. <font size="4"><b>Min depth level</b></font> is filtering by sequencing depth.
You can vary the level using both the plus/minus and the slider.

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/seq_depth.png').default} width="350"/>
</p>

6. <font size="4"><b>Saved filter</b></font>.
You can read how to save filter <a href="/results/variant-viewer/advanced_filtering#saving-a-query">here</a>.
To see a list of your saved filters, click on the "-" line:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/vv/saved_query_options.png').default} width="170"/>
</p>

### Filtering reset

To clear ***all*** filters, click on <img src={require('/img/vv/clear_filters.png').default} width="40"/>.
To reset an ***individual filter***:
- select "**All**" option (to reset ORIGIN, GENE PANEL, GENE)
- click on the "**cross**" (to reset HPO)
- select “**-**” (to reset SAVED FILTER)
