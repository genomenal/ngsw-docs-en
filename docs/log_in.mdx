# Login to the System

When you first open Genomenal, you will see a login page:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/eng/log_in_page.png').default} width="1000"/>
</p>

### Sign In {#sign-in}

If you **already have an account**, enter your login or email and password that you used when registering
in the system, and click on the button <img src={require('/img/eng/login_btn.png').default} width="300"/>.

### Change Password {#change-password}

If you **forgot your password**, click on the "*Forgot password?*" link, enter your login or email that you
used when registering in the system, and
click on <img src={require('/img/eng/reset_password.png').default} width="300"/>:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/eng/change_pswd.png').default} width="350"/>
</p>

Then go to your mailbox that you used when registering. You will receive an email with a link to change
your password. By opening the link, you will see the password change page:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/eng/change_password_form.png').default} width="350"/>
</p>

Enter the new password twice and click on <img src={require('/img/eng/save.png').default} width="300"/>.

### Sign Up {#sign_up}

If you don't have a Genomenal account yet, register in the system:
- If you received **an email with an invitation** from Genomenal, follow the registration link
provided in the email. You will see a page for registering in the system:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/eng/signup_through_invitation.png').default} width="350"/>
</p>

1. By default, the "*Email*" field will contain the email address to which the invitation letter
was sent. You can change it if you like. A link will then be sent to this address to confirm registration.
2. Create and enter a login and password. You will use them when you sign in later. Login and password
can contain any characters. Login can consist of no more than 32 characters.
3. Check the box to accept the terms of use and privacy policy.
4. Click on <img src={require('/img/eng/signup_btn.png').default} width="300"/>.

You will be see your Genomenal account. In the future, use your login and password to sign in, as
described [above](/log_in#sign-in).

- If you have **an invitation code**, click on the "*Sign Up*" link to open the registration page:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/eng/signup_page.png').default} width="350"/>
</p>

1. Enter your email. You will then receive a link to complete your registration.
2. Create and enter a login and password. You will use them when you sign in later.
Login and password can contain any characters. Login can consist of no more than 32 characters.
3. Enter the invitation code.
4. Check the box to accept the terms of use and privacy policy.
5. Click on <img src={require('/img/eng/signup_btn.png').default} width="300"/>.

You will see a confirmation page for the entered email:

<p align="center">
<img src={require('/img/eng/confirm_email.png').default} width="350"/>
</p>

By clicking "*Back*", you will return to the login page. Go to your mailbox that you used while registering.
There you will receive an email with a link to complete registration in the system.
By clicking on the link, you will see your Genomenal account. In the future, use your
login and password to sign in, as described [above](/log_in#sign-in).

- If you didn't receive an invitation letter and you don't have an invitation code, please
contact [info@genomenal.com](mailto:info@genomenal.com).
