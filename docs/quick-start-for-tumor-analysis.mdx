### 3. Upload Samples {#upload-samples}

Select tumor sample file(s) to analyze. Commonly, the sample is a paired-end sequencing data in FASTQ format.

:::caution Attention!
If you have a matched non-tumor tissue sample of the same patient, then a control assay
is much more reliable.
:::

Upload tumor sample(s) into the "TUMOR TISSUE" window and normal sample (if available) into the "MATCHED NON-TUMOR
TISSUE" window:

![Загрузка файлов в формате FASTQ](/img/eng/TN_upl.png)
