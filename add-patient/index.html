Adding Patient

info

A Patient, or Proband, is a person from whom samples were taken for analysis.

In case you want to upload samples for a patient who is not yet in the system, click on "Add Patient" to create a patient entry.

Only the Patient ID field is required. You can fill out the details later on this patient's personal page. Click on to proceed.

You will see the patient page.
